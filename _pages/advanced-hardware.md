        <h2 style="font-weight: 800; margin-bottom: 1.5rem; display: flex; align-items: center;"><i class="fa-solid fa-wifi fa-lg mr-3" style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"></i> The Concept</h2>
        <p>
          To properly correct for motion and physiology, the MRI scanner must understand exactly what the patient is doing at any given millisecond. Often, relying on the MRI signal itself for physiological tracking is insufficient or causes extreme computational overhead. 
        </p>
        <p class="mb-0">
          Our solution is the integration of <strong>Smart Sensors</strong>—such as ultra-wideband radar, optical cameras, and wireless respiratory belts—directly into the MRI suite. These advanced, MR-safe devices capture respiratory and cardiac cycles seamlessly in real-time, providing instantaneous feedback to the scanner orchestrating perfect image reconstruction without inconveniencing the patient.
        </p>
