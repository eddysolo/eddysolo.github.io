Dr. Eddy Solomon leads Solomon's MRI Lab at the Technion, where his group develops advanced MRI acquisition, reconstruction, and analysis methods.

His research combines magnetic resonance physics with machine learning to improve image quality, accelerate exams, and increase diagnostic value in breast and oncologic imaging.
