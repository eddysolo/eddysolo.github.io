New Publication in Nature Communications, 2026: We are pleased to announce the publication of our latest research, ["Dynamic breast MRI with Flexible Temporal Resolution Aided by Deep Learning"](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-026-72776-z) in *Nature Communications*. In this work, we present ELITE, a deep-learning reconstruction framework that combines artificial intelligence with mathematical modeling to produce fast dynamic MRI for breast cancer imaging at up to one frame per second.
