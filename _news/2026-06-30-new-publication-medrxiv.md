New Publication in medRxiv, 2026: We are pleased to announce the publication of our latest research, ["Software-defined Radar for MRI Motion Correction: A versatile, vendor-independent Platform"](https://www.medrxiv.org/content/10.64898/2026.05.16.26351399v1) in *medRxiv*. This work introduces a vendor-independent platform for MRI motion correction using software-defined radar, expanding our toolkit for robust imaging in the presence of motion.
